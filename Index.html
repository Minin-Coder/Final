Our Mission

What is Elementz_Live ?

  • • The Elementz_Live is a Multi-day, Multi-part event, consisting of a summit, a high-level competition of the arts in different genres, Live Comedy, live‑in‑concert performances, and a social mixer.

  • • Our most important objective is to provide a real opportunity to artists in a space where they can freely express themselves while focusing on discovering their true potential.

  • • We intend to educate people’s perceptions about hip‑hop, the business, and music culture as a whole using our brand and competition as tools.

  • • The Elementz_Live exposes young artists to different genres of art and culture while providing education and new technologies specific to their artistic goals.

  • • We're developing and perfecting our system by implementing new ideas into The Elementz_Live each year.
